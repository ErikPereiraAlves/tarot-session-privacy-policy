Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile game Tarot Session, published by ERIK ALVES.

1. Overview

Tarot Session is a mobile game application. The app does not require account registration and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or home address.

2. Information We Collect

Tarot Session itself does not directly collect personal data from users.

However, depending on the version of the app and the services enabled in the distributed build, certain third-party services may process limited technical information, such as:

  • device type and operating system version;
  • app version;
  • crash logs and diagnostics;
  • performance data;
  • advertising identifiers;
  • aggregated usage information.

3. How Information Is Used

Any technical information processed through the app is used only for purposes such as:

  • operating the app correctly;
  • improving stability and performance;
  • detecting and fixing crashes or technical issues;
  • measuring app usage in an aggregated way;
  • serving ads, if advertising is enabled in the app.

4. Third-Party Services

Depending on the published version of Tarot Session, the app may use third-party services such as Google Play services, Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics, or Firebase Crashlytics.

These services may process limited technical data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

If third-party SDKs or platform services are enabled, limited technical information may be processed by those providers strictly for analytics, diagnostics, fraud prevention, app functionality, or advertising support, where applicable.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Tarot Session does not provide in-app user accounts and does not maintain a user account database.

Where technical data is processed by third-party providers, retention and deletion are handled according to their own policies and legal obligations.

7. Children's Privacy

Tarot Session is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so that we can review the matter.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information associated with the operation of the app and rely on established platform and service-provider protections where applicable.

9. Your Choices

You may stop all collection related to the app by uninstalling it. You may also manage relevant privacy, permissions, and advertising settings through your device or Google account settings, where available.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page together with a revised "Last updated" date.
